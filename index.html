Hi! I'm Margot Rysman, currently 3rd year Industrial Design student at Ghent University in Kortrijk. I live around Bruges and studied there for my secondary education at the Sint-Jozefshumaniora, where I studied Latin and Math.

I've always loved making and destroying them, wanting to find out for myself how they work. I followed that passion throughout my youth, participating in technical clubs at school like T4U where I printed, sawed and glued to my hearts content. And now I get to continue it as a job! What a life to live.

In this portfolio I aim to collect some records of my projects and skills here, so you can see what I can do with what I learned up until now. Hopefully it'll be ever expanding, you're never too old to keep learning! Even this site is teaching me new things, such as programming html and CSS.
I wouldn't want it any other way!
